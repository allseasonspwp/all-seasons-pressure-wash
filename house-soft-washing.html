Frequently Asked Questions

House Soft Washing FAQs

What is soft washing?

Soft washing uses lower pressure and selected cleaning solutions to treat organic buildup on exterior surfaces.

Will a house wash remove gutter tiger stripes?

Not always. Oxidation lines on gutters often require a separate gutter-brightening service.

Do I need to be home?

Many exterior projects can be completed without the homeowner present when access, water and project details are confirmed.
