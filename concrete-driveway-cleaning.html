Frequently Asked Questions

Concrete & Driveway Cleaning FAQs

Can every stain be removed completely?

No service can guarantee complete removal of every oil, rust or deeply embedded stain. We explain realistic expectations before work begins.

Can you clean patios and walkways too?

Yes. We clean many durable concrete and masonry surfaces, depending on condition and compatibility.

Will the concrete look brand new?

Cleaning can produce a major improvement, but age, wear, previous damage and permanent staining can still remain.
