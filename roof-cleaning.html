Frequently Asked Questions

Roof Cleaning FAQs

Do you pressure wash asphalt shingles?

No. Asphalt shingles should be treated with a low-pressure roof-cleaning process rather than aggressive pressure washing.

Will moss disappear immediately?

Some staining responds quickly, while heavier moss and lichen may continue releasing naturally after treatment.

How often should a roof be cleaned?

Timing depends on shade, moisture, nearby trees and the roof's condition. We can recommend a practical maintenance interval after inspecting the property.
